Don't let media perceptions deter you from flying!

Public opinion has been swayed by recent news about air travel safety. As we go depth in data, we could see that travel with American Airlines is safer.

American Airlines (AA) considers passenger's safety as its first priority. Let us view some facts.

This All Airlines Fatal Accidents & Incidents (1985 - 2014) chart tells us how many Fatal Accidents and Incidents occurred for every airlines across the world during the 30-year time period. 

This Passenger Flights - Accidents Over the Years graphs shows us the decreasing trend of Accidents over the years for passenger flights. 

This Fatalities Over the Years graphs shows us the decreasing trend of Accidents over the years for passenger flights. 

As we see, there's significant decrease in fatal accidents and incidents over the years.

In addition to this, AirlineRatings.com rates American Airlines with a 7/7 for safety. Here's what AirlineRatings looks at when rating:

  • Whether the airline has had a fatal crash in the last 10 years.

  • Whether the airline has suffered numerous serious pilot related incidents.

  • Whether the airline and its country of origin passed major audits.

  • Whether the airline is complying with international COVID-19 standards.

The Global Biorisk Advisory Council has awarded American Airlines the GBAC STAR™ Accreditation for health safety. This indicates that the airline has implemented a cleaning, disinfection, and disease prevention program. If you're worried about traveling because of the epidemic, this information may put your mind at ease.

 

When flying with American Airlines, you can rest assured that you will be safe.
